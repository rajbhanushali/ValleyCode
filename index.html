ValleyCode

San Ramon's first computer science competition.

Learn More

Not your ordinary hackathon

Teams of 2-3 will be given various computer science related problems and challenges to solve. More difficult challenges are worth more points, and the goal is to get the most points in the 6 hour time limit. For now, the language to solve to problem in is Java.

How it Works

Get your challenge!

At the beginning of the competition, teams will get one packet with problems of ascending difficulty, each with their own respective point values. It is COMPLETELY up to your team to decide which problems to do and what order to do them in. Just keep in mind the time limit!

Solve the Problem!

Work with your team to write Java code that will solve the problem. Teams will have access ONLY to the Javadocs and other basic references.

Submit your answer!

Once you and your team have built a working piece of code, you can submit it into our autonomous code checking system. The main goal of your code is to have it working, but using a faster method will also give you the upper hand.

Rinse and Repeat!

If the code checking system gives your code the green light, then you are ready to move on and the points will be given to your team! You can then repeat this process for as many problems as you can until the time limit runs out. If the code checking system says there's something wrong with your code, don't worry! You can make changes to your code and resubmit it as many times as you'd like.

FAQ

Who can come?

This event is for high school students in the San Ramon Valley area. A basic knowledge of Computer Science is highly recommended. If you are in middle school or from a nearby area, contact us.

Where and when is this?

We are currently in the process of finding a venue and acquiring sponsors. Make sure to sign up with your email, so we can get an idea of how many people will attend and keep you posted for future updates as well!

What should I bring?

Have one person from your team bring a laptop. If your team has over one laptop, the others will be kept seperately. Snacks may be helpful to keep your team motivated, but they may be supplied here. Since the event only lasts for 7 hours or so, there is no need to bring any sleeping bags, toiletries, and such.

I am interested in sponsoring your event. What should I do?

If you are interested in providing a venue, send us an email. Want to sponsor us? Click the button below, fill out the form, and tell us more about yourself!
